Check how many endpoints the Service has:

  kubectl get endpoints myapp-svc -n ckaquest

You'll see only 3 IPs — all from v1 pods. The v2 pod is missing.

Now check the Service selector:

  kubectl get svc myapp-svc -n ckaquest -o jsonpath='{.spec.selector}'

It selects on: app=myapp. Check if v2 pods have that label:

  kubectl get pods -n ckaquest -l version=v2 --show-labels
