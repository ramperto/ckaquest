Since deployment selectors are immutable, delete and recreate app-v2:

  kubectl delete deploy app-v2 -n ckaquest

  kubectl apply -f - <<EOF
  apiVersion: apps/v1
  kind: Deployment
  metadata:
    name: app-v2
    namespace: ckaquest
    labels:
      app: myapp
      version: v2
  spec:
    replicas: 1
    selector:
      matchLabels:
        app: myapp
        version: v2
    template:
      metadata:
        labels:
          app: myapp
          version: v2
      spec:
        containers:
          - name: nginx
            image: nginx:1.25
            env:
              - name: APP_VERSION
                value: "v2"
  EOF

Verify: kubectl get endpoints myapp-svc -n ckaquest
You should now see 4 IPs.
