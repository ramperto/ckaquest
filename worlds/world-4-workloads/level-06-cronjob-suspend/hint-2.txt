Unsuspend with patch:

  kubectl patch cronjob report-generator -n ckaquest \
    -p '{"spec":{"suspend":false}}'

Verify:
  kubectl get cronjob report-generator -n ckaquest
  # SUSPEND column: False

Now trigger a manual run (don't wait 5 minutes for the schedule):
  kubectl create job report-now --from=cronjob/report-generator -n ckaquest
