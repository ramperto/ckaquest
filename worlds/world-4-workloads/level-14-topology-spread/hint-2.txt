The topology key `topology.kubernetes.io/rack` doesn't exist on any node.
Check what labels your nodes actually have:

  kubectl get nodes --show-labels

Common topology keys that exist:
  - kubernetes.io/hostname       (always exists)
  - topology.kubernetes.io/zone  (cloud providers)
  - topology.kubernetes.io/region (cloud providers)

Change the topologyKey to `kubernetes.io/hostname` which is always present.

On a single-node cluster, also change `whenUnsatisfiable` from
`DoNotSchedule` to `ScheduleAnyway` since all pods land on the same node.
