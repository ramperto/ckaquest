Check why no pods are scheduled:

  kubectl get daemonset node-monitor -n ckaquest
  # desiredNumberScheduled: 0

  kubectl describe daemonset node-monitor -n ckaquest | grep -A5 "Node-Selector"
  # You'll see: disktype=ssd

Check if any node has this label:
  kubectl get nodes --show-labels | grep disktype

No match — the nodeSelector is filtering out all nodes.
