Two ways to fix this:

Option A — Remove the nodeSelector (DaemonSet runs on all nodes):
  kubectl patch daemonset node-monitor -n ckaquest \
    --type=json \
    -p='[{"op":"remove","path":"/spec/template/spec/nodeSelector"}]'

Option B — Label the node to match:
  NODE=$(kubectl get nodes -o jsonpath='{.items[0].name}')
  kubectl label node $NODE disktype=ssd

After either fix, check:
  kubectl get daemonset node-monitor -n ckaquest
  # desiredNumberScheduled should now be 1+
