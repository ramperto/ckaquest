Easiest fix — delete and recreate without the nodeSelector:

  kubectl delete daemonset node-monitor -n ckaquest

  kubectl apply -f - <<EOF
  apiVersion: apps/v1
  kind: DaemonSet
  metadata:
    name: node-monitor
    namespace: ckaquest
  spec:
    selector:
      matchLabels:
        app: node-monitor
    template:
      metadata:
        labels:
          app: node-monitor
      spec:
        tolerations:
          - operator: Exists
        containers:
          - name: monitor
            image: busybox:1.36
            command: ["sh", "-c", "while true; do echo monitoring; sleep 30; done"]
  EOF

Wait for the pod to start:
  kubectl get pods -n ckaquest -l app=node-monitor

Then: validate
