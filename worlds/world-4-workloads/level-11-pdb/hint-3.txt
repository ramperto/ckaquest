PDBs cannot be edited in-place for spec changes. Delete and recreate:

  kubectl delete pdb pdb-app-pdb -n ckaquest

  kubectl apply -f - <<EOF
  apiVersion: policy/v1
  kind: PodDisruptionBudget
  metadata:
    name: pdb-app-pdb
    namespace: ckaquest
  spec:
    maxUnavailable: 1
    selector:
      matchLabels:
        app: pdb-app
  EOF

Verify: kubectl get pdb pdb-app-pdb -n ckaquest
The ALLOWED column should now show 1.
