Create the missing PriorityClass:

  kubectl apply -f - <<EOF
  apiVersion: scheduling.k8s.io/v1
  kind: PriorityClass
  metadata:
    name: high-priority
  value: 1000000
  globalDefault: false
  description: "High priority for critical workloads"
  EOF

PriorityClass is a CLUSTER-scoped resource — no namespace needed.

After creating it, the existing pending pod may or may not auto-reschedule.
To be safe, delete and recreate the pod:
  kubectl delete pod critical-app -n ckaquest
  kubectl apply -f <original-broken.yaml>
