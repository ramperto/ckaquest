Check why the pod is Pending:

  kubectl describe pod gpu-worker -n ckaquest | grep -A5 "Events:"
  # "0/1 nodes are available: 1 node(s) had untolerated taint {gpu: present}"

Check the node taint:
  kubectl describe node $(kubectl get nodes -o jsonpath='{.items[0].name}') \
    | grep Taints
  # Taints: gpu=present:NoSchedule

A NoSchedule taint means: "do NOT schedule pods here unless they
explicitly tolerate this taint."
