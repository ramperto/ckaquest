Pods can't be edited in place to add tolerations. Delete and recreate:

  kubectl delete pod gpu-worker -n ckaquest

  kubectl apply -f - <<EOF
  apiVersion: v1
  kind: Pod
  metadata:
    name: gpu-worker
    namespace: ckaquest
    labels:
      app: gpu-worker
  spec:
    tolerations:
      - key: "gpu"
        value: "present"
        effect: "NoSchedule"
    containers:
      - name: worker
        image: busybox:1.36
        command: ["sh", "-c", "echo 'GPU training started'; sleep 3600"]
  EOF

The toleration must match: key=gpu, value=present, effect=NoSchedule.
