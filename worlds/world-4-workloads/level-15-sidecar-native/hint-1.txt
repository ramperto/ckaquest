Check the pod status:

  kubectl get pod app-with-sidecar -n ckaquest

You'll see: STATUS = Init:0/1 — the init container is running but
never completing. The main container hasn't started yet.

Describe the pod to see more detail:

  kubectl describe pod app-with-sidecar -n ckaquest

Look at the Init Containers section — log-agent is in "Running" state
but the main container is "Waiting" (PodInitializing).
