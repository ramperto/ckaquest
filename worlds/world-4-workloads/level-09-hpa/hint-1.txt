Check the HPA status:

  kubectl get hpa web-api-hpa -n ckaquest
  # TARGETS         MINPODS   MAXPODS
  # <unknown>/50%   1         5

"<unknown>" means the HPA can't read CPU utilization.

Why? HPA calculates CPU% as:
  (current_cpu_usage) / (cpu_request) * 100

If there's no cpu_request, there's no denominator — result is unknown.

Check the deployment:
  kubectl get deployment web-api -n ckaquest \
    -o jsonpath='{.spec.template.spec.containers[0].resources}'
  # {} — empty, no requests!
