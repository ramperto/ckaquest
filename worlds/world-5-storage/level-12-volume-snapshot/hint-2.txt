Hint 2: Create the VolumeSnapshotClass

You need to create a VolumeSnapshotClass with:
- apiVersion: snapshot.storage.k8s.io/v1
- kind: VolumeSnapshotClass
- name: csi-snap-class
- driver: rancher.io/local-path (matches the cluster's CSI driver)
- deletionPolicy: Delete

Use `kubectl apply -f -` and paste the YAML.
