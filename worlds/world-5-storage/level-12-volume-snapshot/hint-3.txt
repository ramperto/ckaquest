Hint 3: Full Solution

Create the VolumeSnapshotClass:

  cat <<EOF | kubectl apply -f -
  apiVersion: snapshot.storage.k8s.io/v1
  kind: VolumeSnapshotClass
  metadata:
    name: csi-snap-class
  driver: rancher.io/local-path
  deletionPolicy: Delete
  EOF

Then reapply the VolumeSnapshot if needed:

  cat <<EOF | kubectl apply -f -
  apiVersion: snapshot.storage.k8s.io/v1
  kind: VolumeSnapshot
  metadata:
    name: data-snapshot
    namespace: ckaquest
  spec:
    volumeSnapshotClassName: csi-snap-class
    source:
      persistentVolumeClaimName: source-data
  EOF

Verify:

  kubectl get volumesnapshotclass csi-snap-class
  kubectl get volumesnapshot data-snapshot -n ckaquest
