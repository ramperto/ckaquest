Create the missing ConfigMap:

  kubectl create configmap app-config -n ckaquest \
    --from-literal=app.properties=$'log_level=INFO\nmax_connections=100'

OR using YAML:
  kubectl apply -f - <<EOF
  apiVersion: v1
  kind: ConfigMap
  metadata:
    name: app-config
    namespace: ckaquest
  data:
    app.properties: |
      log_level=INFO
      max_connections=100
  EOF

Once the ConfigMap exists, the kubelet will automatically retry mounting it.
Check the pod — it should transition from ContainerCreating → Running.
