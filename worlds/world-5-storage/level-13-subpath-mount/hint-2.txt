Hint 2: Use subPath

The `subPath` field in a volumeMount lets you mount a single file from
a volume without replacing the entire directory.

Change the volumeMount to include subPath:

  volumeMounts:
    - name: config-vol
      mountPath: /etc/app/app.conf
      subPath: app.conf

Note: mountPath changes from `/etc/app` (directory) to `/etc/app/app.conf`
(specific file), and subPath specifies which key from the ConfigMap to mount.
