PVC spec is immutable — delete and recreate with the correct size:

  kubectl delete pvc db-data -n ckaquest

  kubectl apply -f - <<EOF
  apiVersion: v1
  kind: PersistentVolumeClaim
  metadata:
    name: db-data
    namespace: ckaquest
  spec:
    accessModes:
      - ReadWriteOnce
    storageClassName: manual
    resources:
      requests:
        storage: 5Gi   # must be <= PV capacity (5Gi)
  EOF

The PVC requests must be <= PV capacity AND the accessModes and
storageClassName must also match.
