Check the PVC status:
  kubectl get pvc db-data -n ckaquest
  # STATUS: Pending

Check why it's Pending:
  kubectl describe pvc db-data -n ckaquest | tail -10
  # "no persistent volumes available for this claim and no storage class is set"
  # OR: "Insufficient capacity..."

Check the available PV:
  kubectl get pv db-pv
  # CAPACITY: 5Gi

The PVC requests 10Gi but the PV only has 5Gi — it will never bind.
