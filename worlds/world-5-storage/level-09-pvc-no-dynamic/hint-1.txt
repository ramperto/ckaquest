Check the PVC:

  kubectl get pvc cache-data -n ckaquest
  # STATUS: Pending

  kubectl get pvc cache-data -n ckaquest \
    -o jsonpath='{.spec.storageClassName}'
  # (empty — but that means "" not omitted)

The crucial difference:
  storageClassName: ""    → I want a PV with NO StorageClass (static only)
  (no storageClassName)   → Use the cluster's default StorageClass (dynamic)

Check the default StorageClass:
  kubectl get storageclass
  # local-path (default)
