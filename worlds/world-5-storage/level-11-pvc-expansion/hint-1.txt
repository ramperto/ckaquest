Hint 1: Identify the Problem

Try expanding the PVC directly:

  kubectl edit pvc app-data -n ckaquest

Change the storage from 500Mi to 2Gi and save. You will get an error
because the StorageClass does not allow volume expansion.

Check the StorageClass:

  kubectl get storageclass no-expand-sc -o yaml

Look at the `allowVolumeExpansion` field — it is set to `false`.
