Hint 3: Expand the PVC

After patching the StorageClass, expand the PVC:

  kubectl edit pvc app-data -n ckaquest

Change the storage request from 500Mi to 2Gi:

  spec:
    resources:
      requests:
        storage: 2Gi

Or use patch:

  kubectl patch pvc app-data -n ckaquest -p '{"spec":{"resources":{"requests":{"storage":"2Gi"}}}}'

Then confirm everything is healthy:

  kubectl get pvc app-data -n ckaquest
  kubectl get deployment data-app -n ckaquest
