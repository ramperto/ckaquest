Hint 1: Understand the Risk

Check the current StorageClass reclaim policy:

  kubectl get storageclass delete-sc -o jsonpath='{.reclaimPolicy}'

It says "Delete". This means when PVC `important-data` is deleted, the
PV backing it will also be deleted — all data is lost forever.

You need to create a safer StorageClass with `reclaimPolicy: Retain`.
