Check the storageClassName mismatch:

  kubectl get pv app-pv -o jsonpath='{.spec.storageClassName}'
  # standard

  kubectl get pvc app-storage -n ckaquest -o jsonpath='{.spec.storageClassName}'
  # fast-ssd

Also verify no StorageClass named fast-ssd exists:
  kubectl get storageclass
  # fast-ssd is not listed

The PVC will wait forever for a PV or dynamic provisioner with name fast-ssd.
