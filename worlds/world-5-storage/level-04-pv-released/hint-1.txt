Check the PV state:

  kubectl get pv data-pv
  # STATUS: Released  (not Available)

  kubectl describe pv data-pv | grep -A3 "Claim:"
  # Claim: ckaquest/temp-claim

With Retain reclaim policy, when a PVC is deleted the PV goes to
"Released" — its data is preserved but it's locked by the old claimRef.
A Released PV cannot be claimed by any new PVC until the claimRef is removed.
