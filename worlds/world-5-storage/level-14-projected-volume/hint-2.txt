Hint 2: Two Typos to Fix

There are two bugs in the projected volume sources:

1. "configmap" should be "configMap" (capital M)
   Kubernetes API uses camelCase. The correct key for a ConfigMap
   source in a projected volume is "configMap", not "configmap".

2. "metadata.label" should be "metadata.labels" (plural)
   The Downward API field for pod labels is "metadata.labels" with
   an 's' at the end.
