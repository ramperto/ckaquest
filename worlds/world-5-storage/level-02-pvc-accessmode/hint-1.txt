Check the mismatch:

  kubectl get pv pipeline-pv -o jsonpath='{.spec.accessModes}'
  # ["ReadWriteOnce"]

  kubectl get pvc pipeline-data -n ckaquest -o jsonpath='{.spec.accessModes}'
  # ["ReadWriteMany"]

For a PVC to bind, its accessModes must be a SUBSET of the PV's accessModes.
ReadWriteMany (RWX) is NOT a subset of ReadWriteOnce (RWO) → Pending.
