The pod is requesting 100 CPUs and 200Gi of memory. This is way more than
your node has. The scheduler event says "Insufficient cpu" or "Insufficient memory".

Check the pod's resource requests:
  kubectl get pod compute -n ckaquest -o jsonpath='{.spec.containers[0].resources}'

Your node probably has 4 CPUs and 4Gi RAM. The request needs to fit within that.
