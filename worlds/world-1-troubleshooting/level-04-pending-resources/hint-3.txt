Delete the broken pod and recreate with sensible resource requests:

  kubectl delete pod compute -n ckaquest

  kubectl run compute --image=nginx:1.25 -n ckaquest \
    --requests=cpu=100m,memory=128Mi \
    --limits=cpu=500m,memory=256Mi

Note: "100m" = 100 millicores = 0.1 CPU. Much more reasonable than "100" = 100 CPUs.
