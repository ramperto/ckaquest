The monitor pod is getting 403 Forbidden when calling the Kubernetes API.
This is an RBAC issue.

Check the existing RBAC resources:
  kubectl get serviceaccounts -n ckaquest
  kubectl get roles -n ckaquest
  kubectl get rolebindings -n ckaquest

Also test permissions directly:
  kubectl auth can-i list pods \
    --as=system:serviceaccount:ckaquest:monitor-sa \
    -n ckaquest
