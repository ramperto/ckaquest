The pod is Running but DNS doesn't work inside it. Test DNS yourself:

  kubectl exec debug-pod -n ckaquest -- nslookup kubernetes.default
  kubectl exec debug-pod -n ckaquest -- nslookup google.com

Also check how DNS is configured on the pod:
  kubectl get pod debug-pod -n ckaquest -o jsonpath='{.spec.dnsPolicy}'
  kubectl get pod debug-pod -n ckaquest -o jsonpath='{.spec.dnsConfig}'
