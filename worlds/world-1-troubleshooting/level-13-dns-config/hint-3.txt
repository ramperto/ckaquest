Delete the broken pod and recreate without the broken DNS config:

  kubectl delete pod debug-pod -n ckaquest

  cat <<EOF | kubectl apply -f -
  apiVersion: v1
  kind: Pod
  metadata:
    name: debug-pod
    namespace: ckaquest
  spec:
    dnsPolicy: ClusterFirst
    containers:
      - name: debug
        image: busybox:1.36
        command: ["sh", "-c", "sleep 3600"]
  EOF

Test DNS:
  kubectl exec debug-pod -n ckaquest -- nslookup kubernetes.default
