Fix 1 — Create the ServiceAccount:
  kubectl create serviceaccount app-runner -n ckaquest

Fix 2 — Edit the Service targetPort:
  kubectl edit svc event-svc -n ckaquest
  (change targetPort from 8080 to 80)

After both fixes, you may need to restart the deployment:
  kubectl rollout restart deployment event-app -n ckaquest

Then verify:
  kubectl get pods -n ckaquest -l app=event-app
  kubectl get endpoints event-svc -n ckaquest
