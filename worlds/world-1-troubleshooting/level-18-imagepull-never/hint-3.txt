Delete and recreate the pod:

  kubectl delete pod local-app -n ckaquest

Then apply a fixed version with image `nginx:1.25` and `imagePullPolicy: IfNotPresent`:

  kubectl apply -f - <<EOF
  apiVersion: v1
  kind: Pod
  metadata:
    name: local-app
    namespace: ckaquest
    labels:
      app: local-app
  spec:
    containers:
      - name: app
        image: nginx:1.25
        imagePullPolicy: IfNotPresent
        ports:
          - containerPort: 80
  EOF
