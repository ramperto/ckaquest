There is a NetworkPolicy "backend-deny-all" that selects pods with
label "app: backend" and has empty ingress rules — denying all ingress.

You need to apply a NEW NetworkPolicy that allows ingress to backend
pods FROM frontend pods on port 80.

The frontend pod has label: app=frontend
The backend pod has label: app=backend

A NetworkPolicy can use podSelector in its "from" field to allow
traffic from specific pods.
