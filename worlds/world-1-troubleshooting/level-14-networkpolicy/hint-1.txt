Traffic from frontend to backend is blocked. Test it yourself:

  BACKEND_IP=$(kubectl get svc backend-svc -n ckaquest -o jsonpath='{.spec.clusterIP}')
  kubectl exec frontend -n ckaquest -- wget -qO- --timeout=3 http://$BACKEND_IP

Also check what NetworkPolicies are in effect:
  kubectl get networkpolicies -n ckaquest
  kubectl describe networkpolicy -n ckaquest
