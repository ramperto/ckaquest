Delete the old secret and create a new one with the correct certificate:

  kubectl delete secret app-tls -n ckaquest
  kubectl create secret tls app-tls \
    --cert=/tmp/tls.crt \
    --key=/tmp/tls.key \
    -n ckaquest

Verify:
  kubectl get secret app-tls -n ckaquest -o jsonpath='{.data.tls\.crt}' | base64 -d | openssl x509 -noout -subject
  # Should show: subject=CN = app.example.com
