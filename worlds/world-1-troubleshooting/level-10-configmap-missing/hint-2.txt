The pod references configmap "app-config" via envFrom, but it doesn't exist.

You need to CREATE the configmap. It just needs to exist with the right name.
The content can be any key=value pairs appropriate for the app.

Quick way:
  kubectl create configmap app-config \
    --from-literal=APP_ENV=production \
    --from-literal=LOG_LEVEL=info \
    -n ckaquest
