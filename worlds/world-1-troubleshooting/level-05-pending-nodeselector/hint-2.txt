The pod has a nodeSelector requiring "disktype: ssd" but no node has that label.

You have two options:
1. Remove the nodeSelector from the pod (simpler — if any node is fine)
2. Add the label to your node (if the pod genuinely needs SSD storage)

Check node name:
  kubectl get nodes

Option 2 - add label:
  kubectl label node <node-name> disktype=ssd
