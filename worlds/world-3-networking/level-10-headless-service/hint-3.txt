StatefulSet pods also get individual stable DNS names via the headless Service:

  <pod-name>.<service-name>.<namespace>.svc.cluster.local

For this exercise:
  db-cluster-0.db-headless.ckaquest.svc.cluster.local
  db-cluster-1.db-headless.ckaquest.svc.cluster.local
  db-cluster-2.db-headless.ckaquest.svc.cluster.local

Test individual pod resolution:
  kubectl exec client -n ckaquest -- nslookup db-cluster-0.db-headless.ckaquest.svc.cluster.local
  kubectl exec client -n ckaquest -- nslookup db-cluster-1.db-headless.ckaquest.svc.cluster.local

If the StatefulSet pods are slow to start (postgres pulls image):
  kubectl get pods -n ckaquest -l app=db-cluster

Wait until all show Running 1/1, then run:
  validate
