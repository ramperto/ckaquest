Easiest fix: delete the pod and recreate with dnsPolicy: ClusterFirst,
removing the dnsConfig section entirely:

  kubectl delete pod dns-client -n ckaquest

Then apply a fixed manifest:

  apiVersion: v1
  kind: Pod
  metadata:
    name: dns-client
    namespace: ckaquest
    labels:
      app: dns-client
  spec:
    containers:
      - name: client
        image: busybox:1.36
        command: ["sleep", "3600"]
    dnsPolicy: ClusterFirst

  kubectl apply -f fixed-dns-client.yaml

Verify:
  kubectl exec dns-client -n ckaquest -- nslookup web-svc.ckaquest.svc.cluster.local
