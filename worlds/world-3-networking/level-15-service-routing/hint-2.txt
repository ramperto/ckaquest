There are TWO issues:

1. Service selector has a typo: `app: frontned` should be `app: frontend`
   This alone causes empty endpoints.

2. Readiness probe checks path `/healthz` which nginx doesn't serve
   (returns 404). Pods are never marked Ready. Even with a correct
   selector, unready pods are excluded from endpoints.

You need to fix BOTH issues for traffic to flow.

Check readiness probe failures:
  kubectl describe pod -n ckaquest -l app=frontend | grep -A5 "Readiness"
