Check endpoints first — are they empty?

  kubectl get endpoints frontend-svc -n ckaquest

If empty, compare the service selector with pod labels:

  kubectl get svc frontend-svc -n ckaquest -o jsonpath='{.spec.selector}'
  kubectl get pods -n ckaquest -l app=frontend --show-labels

Also check if the pods are Ready:

  kubectl get pods -n ckaquest -l app=frontend

If pods show 0/1 READY, the readiness probe is failing — and unready
pods are excluded from Service endpoints.
