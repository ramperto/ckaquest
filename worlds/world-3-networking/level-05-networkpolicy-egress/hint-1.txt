The app pod can't reach the database. Check if it's a network policy issue:

  kubectl get networkpolicies -n ckaquest
  kubectl describe networkpolicy app-egress-deny-all -n ckaquest

Test directly from the app pod:
  kubectl exec app -n ckaquest -- nc -z -w 3 db-svc 5432; echo $?
  kubectl exec app -n ckaquest -- nslookup db-svc

What's blocked? Port 5432? DNS (port 53)? Both?
