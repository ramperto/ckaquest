The Ingress references a TLS Secret named 'webapp-tls' but it doesn't exist.

Verify:
  kubectl get secret -n ckaquest | grep tls
  kubectl describe ingress webapp-ingress -n ckaquest

The Ingress needs a Secret of type kubernetes.io/tls containing:
  - tls.crt (the certificate)
  - tls.key (the private key)

You'll need to generate a certificate first.
