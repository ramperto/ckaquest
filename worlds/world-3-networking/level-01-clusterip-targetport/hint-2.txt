The Service has targetPort: 8080 but nginx listens on port 80.
kube-proxy routes traffic to pod-ip:targetPort — so traffic goes to
pod-ip:8080 which has nothing listening → connection refused.

Patch the service:
  kubectl patch svc backend-svc -n ckaquest \
    --type='json' \
    -p='[{"op":"replace","path":"/spec/ports/0/targetPort","value":80}]'
