platform-viewer aggregates ClusterRoles that have:
  label: rbac.ckaquest.io/aggregate-to-platform-viewer=true

events-reader has this label → its rules are in platform-viewer ✓
logs-reader is MISSING this label → its rules are NOT included ✗

To fix, add the label to logs-reader:
  kubectl label clusterrole logs-reader \
    rbac.ckaquest.io/aggregate-to-platform-viewer=true

Kubernetes will automatically update platform-viewer's rules.
