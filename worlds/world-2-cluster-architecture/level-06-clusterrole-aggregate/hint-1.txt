platform-sa can't list pods even though a 'logs-reader' ClusterRole exists
with those permissions.

Check what rules platform-viewer actually has after aggregation:
  kubectl describe clusterrole platform-viewer

Also check the aggregation rule — what label is it looking for?
  kubectl get clusterrole platform-viewer -o yaml

Then check whether logs-reader has that label:
  kubectl get clusterrole logs-reader -o yaml | grep labels -A5
