Apply an updated quota with higher limits:

  kubectl apply -f - <<EOF
  apiVersion: v1
  kind: ResourceQuota
  metadata:
    name: tight-quota
    namespace: ckaquest
  spec:
    hard:
      pods: "5"
      requests.cpu: "500m"
      requests.memory: "512Mi"
      limits.cpu: "1"
      limits.memory: "1Gi"
  EOF

The ReplicaSet controller will automatically create the 3rd pod once
the quota allows it. Watch it appear:
  kubectl get pods -n ckaquest -w
