The kubeadm upgrade has a strict order. On the CKA exam, getting the
sequence wrong means the upgrade fails or the node gets stuck.

The upgrade always has two phases:
1. Control plane (kubeadm upgrade apply)
2. Node components (kubelet + kubectl)

You can't upgrade kubelet before kubeadm has updated the control plane.

Write your answer to /tmp/upgrade-plan.sh
The key tools are: apt-mark, apt-get, kubeadm, kubectl drain/uncordon
