Static pods are managed by kubelet directly, not via kubectl.
Their manifests live at /etc/kubernetes/manifests/ on the node.

Look at the manifest:
  sudo cat /etc/kubernetes/manifests/infra-monitor.yaml

And check if the pod appears (with a node-name suffix):
  kubectl get pods -n kube-system | grep infra-monitor

Check kubelet logs for errors about this pod:
  sudo journalctl -u k3s -n 50 | grep infra-monitor
