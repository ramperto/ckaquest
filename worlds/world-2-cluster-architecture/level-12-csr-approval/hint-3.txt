Verify the CSR is approved and the certificate was issued:

  kubectl get csr dev-user-csr

Status should show "Approved,Issued".

To check the certificate content:

  kubectl get csr dev-user-csr -o jsonpath='{.status.conditions[0].type}'

Should output "Approved".

To extract the certificate:

  kubectl get csr dev-user-csr -o jsonpath='{.status.certificate}' | base64 -d > /tmp/dev-user.crt
