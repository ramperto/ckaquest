Check what permissions app-deployer currently has:

  kubectl auth can-i list pods \
    --as=system:serviceaccount:ckaquest:app-deployer \
    -n ckaquest

It should say "no" — because no Role or RoleBinding exists yet.

You can also check all permissions:

  kubectl auth can-i --list \
    --as=system:serviceaccount:ckaquest:app-deployer \
    -n ckaquest
