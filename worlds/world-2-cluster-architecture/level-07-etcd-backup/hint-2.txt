k3s saves snapshots to /var/lib/rancher/k3s/server/db/snapshots/
You need to copy it to /opt/etcd-backup.db

  sudo k3s etcd-snapshot save --name cka-backup
  sudo cp /var/lib/rancher/k3s/server/db/snapshots/cka-backup* /opt/etcd-backup.db

On the CKA exam (kubeadm clusters), you'd use etcdctl instead:
  ETCDCTL_API=3 etcdctl snapshot save /opt/etcd-backup.db \
    --endpoints=https://127.0.0.1:2379 \
    --cacert=/etc/kubernetes/pki/etcd/ca.crt \
    --cert=/etc/kubernetes/pki/etcd/server.crt \
    --key=/etc/kubernetes/pki/etcd/server.key
