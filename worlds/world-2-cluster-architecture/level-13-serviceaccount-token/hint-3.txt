Full solution:

  kubectl delete pod api-test -n ckaquest

Then recreate without the automountServiceAccountToken: false line
(or change it to true):

  kubectl run api-test -n ckaquest --image=curlimages/curl:latest \
    --overrides='{"spec":{"serviceAccountName":"api-client","containers":[{"name":"curl","image":"curlimages/curl:latest","command":["sleep","3600"]}]}}' \
    --command -- sleep 3600

Or edit /tmp/api-test-fix.yaml and kubectl apply -f it.

Verify: kubectl exec api-test -n ckaquest -- ls /var/run/secrets/kubernetes.io/serviceaccount/
