Two issues block the drain:
1. A DaemonSet pod (log-agent) — add --ignore-daemonsets
2. A standalone pod with no ReplicaSet/Deployment — add --force
   If it uses emptyDir volumes — also add --delete-emptydir-data

Full command:
  kubectl drain $NODE \
    --ignore-daemonsets \
    --delete-emptydir-data \
    --force

After "maintenance", you must uncordon the node to restore scheduling.
